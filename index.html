About Me

Known by everyone as Uday, I have hobbies that include playing badminton, exploring the world, and learning new things. My ultimate goal is to establish a career in the software development field.

My first professional role was as a Senior Software Tester at Capgemini, where I honed my skills and developed a deep appreciation for teamwork. My journey so far has been shaped by a blend of academic achievements and professional experiences, all driven by my love for collaboration and innovation in the tech world.
